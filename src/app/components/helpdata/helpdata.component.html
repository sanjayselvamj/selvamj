Login Help Section

Login Process Overview

  1. User Enters Login Details

    The user enters their username (at least 4 characters) and password (at least 6 characters) in the login form. The form ensures both fields are filled before submission.

  2. Submit the Form

    The user clicks the "Login" button, and the app collects the entered username and password.

  3. Authentication Request

    The app sends the credentials to the backend, which checks for validity.

  4. Handle the Response

    On success, the backend sends a token or userId to the frontend. The app stores this data in localStorage for session management.

  5. Navigate to Home Page

    After successful login, the user is redirected to the home page automatically.

  6. Login Failure

    If the login fails, an error message is displayed, prompting the user to try again.

Registration Help Section

New User Registration Process

  1. User Enters Registration Details

    The user fills in details like username (at least 4 characters) and password (at least 6 characters) in the registration form.

  2. Submit the Registration Form

    The user clicks "Sign Up", and the form data is sent to the backend.

  3. Check for Existing User

    The backend verifies whether the username already exists in the system. If it does, registration fails.

  4. Handle the Response

    On success, the backend creates a new user account and confirms registration.

  5. Navigate to Login Page

    After successful registration, the user is automatically redirected to the login page.

  6. Registration Failure

    If registration fails, an error message is shown, and the user can attempt to register again.

Creating a Post

To create a new post, fill in the Title and Content fields in the post creation form.

Once you've entered your details, click the "Create Post" button. Your post will then be saved and displayed below the form.

Commenting on Posts

To comment on a post, locate the post you want to comment on. Underneath the post content, you will find an input field labeled "Add a comment."

Type your comment in the field and click the "Comment" button. Your comment will be added to the post and visible to other users.

Deleting a Post

If you wish to delete a post that you have created, click the "Delete Post" button located below the post. A confirmation may be required to ensure you want to proceed with the deletion.

Once deleted, the post will no longer be visible to you or other users.

Sharing a Post

To share a post, click the "Share Post" button beneath the post. This feature allows you to copy a link to the post or share it directly through your preferred social media platforms.

Sharing helps to spread the word about your thoughts and contributions!

Commenting on a Post

To add a comment to a post, navigate to the post you wish to comment on.

In the "Add a Comment" section below the post content, enter your comment in the provided text box.

After writing your comment, click the "Comment" button to submit it. Your comment will now appear under the post for others to see!

Deleting Your Comment

If you decide to delete your comment, find the comment you want to remove.

Next to your comment, you will see a "Delete" button. Click this button to remove your comment.

You may be prompted to confirm the deletion. Once confirmed, your comment will be permanently removed from the post.

Deleting a Post

To delete a post you have created, locate the post on your profile or the main feed.

Below the post, click the "Delete Post" button. A confirmation message will appear asking if you're sure you want to delete the post.

If you confirm, the post will be removed from the site and will no longer be visible to anyone.

Sharing a Post

To share a post, look for the "Share Post" button located below the post content.

Clicking this button will give you options to share the post via a direct link or on social media platforms such as Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram.

Sharing your post helps to reach a wider audience and engage more users in the conversation!
